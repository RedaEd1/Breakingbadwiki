Skyler White

Summary

Skyler White is a complex character from the TV series "Breaking Bad." She is portrayed as the wife of the show's protagonist, Walter White, and is initially introduced as a devoted and caring wife and mother. Skyler's character undergoes significant development throughout the series as she becomes increasingly entangled in her husband's illegal drug manufacturing business. She grapples with ethical dilemmas, trying to protect her family while confronting the dark and morally ambiguous world that Walter has entered. Her character is known for her resilience, intelligence, and ability to adapt to the challenging circumstances she faces, making her a pivotal figure in the series. Skyler White is portrayed by actress Anna Gunn, and her performance in "Breaking Bad" earned her critical acclaim and recognition.
